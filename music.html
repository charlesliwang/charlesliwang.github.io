Musical Background

I picked up the guitar in 2007 as a middle schooler desperate to be cool. Inspired by MTV rockstars such as blink-182, Fall Out Boy and My Chemical Romance, I began to prioritize music over every other aspect of my life. Through years of dedication, my public identity became more and more rooted in my musical efforts and growth. By the time I moved away from Shanghai, my best friends and I had left our mark in the international music scene in the city, with the inception of a series of acoustic charity shows and equally intimate hardcore shows.

My own personal musical style is a blend of punk, hardcore, metal and rock, most evidently influenced by the previously mentioned bands, as well as The Wonder Years, August Burns Red, Protest the Hero, and many more.
